Weather Effects

Different temperatures will affect the real-world range differently:

  • -20 degrees Celsius: 50% rated range
  • -10 degree Celsius: 60% rated range
  • 0 degree Celsius: 80% rated range
  • 10 degrees Celsius: 100% rated range
  • 20 degrees Celsius: 115% rated range
  • 30 degrees Celsius: 105% rated range
  • 40 degrees Celsius: 80% rated range

The program obtains the real-world temperature and decides the closest data point to the result. The according effect will be used in calculating the final CO2 consumption. For simplicity, in this model the energy efficiency is inversely proportional to range.
